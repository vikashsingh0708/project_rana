Aloe Vera Plant

Scientific Name: Aloe vera

Family: Asphodelaceae

Type: Medicinal Plant

Description: Aloe Vera is a succulent plant known for its thick, fleshy leaves.

Uses: It is used in skin care, medicine, and health drinks.

Environmental Importance: Aloe Vera improves indoor air quality and requires very little water.

Additional Facts: It grows well in dry and hot climates.
