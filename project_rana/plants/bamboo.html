Bamboo Plant

Scientific Name: Bambusoideae

Family: Poaceae

Type: Grass Plant

Description: Bamboo is a fast-growing plant with strong hollow stems.

Uses: Bamboo is used for construction, furniture, paper, and handicrafts.

Environmental Importance: Bamboo prevents soil erosion and absorbs carbon dioxide.

Additional Facts: It is one of the fastest-growing plants in the world.
