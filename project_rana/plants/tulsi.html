Tulsi Plant

Scientific Name: Ocimum tenuiflorum

Family: Lamiaceae

Type: Medicinal Plant

Description: Tulsi is a small herb widely grown in Indian households. It is known for its strong medicinal and spiritual value.

Uses: Tulsi is used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat cough, cold, fever, and stress-related problems.

Environmental Importance: Tulsi helps purify air and supports insects like bees. It improves local air quality.

Additional Facts: Tulsi requires sunlight and moderate watering for healthy growth.
