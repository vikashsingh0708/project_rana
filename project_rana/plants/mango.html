Mango Tree

Scientific Name: Mangifera indica

Family: Anacardiaceae

Type: Fruit Tree

Description: Mango is a large evergreen tree famous for its sweet and nutritious fruits.

Uses: Mango fruits are eaten fresh or used to make juice, pickles, and sweets.

Environmental Importance: Mango trees provide shade, oxygen, and food for birds and animals.

Additional Facts: Mango is known as the king of fruits.
