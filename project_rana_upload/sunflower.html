Sunflower Plant

Scientific Name: Helianthus annuus

Family: Asteraceae

Type: Flowering Plant

Description: Sunflower is a tall plant known for its bright yellow flowers that face the sun.

Uses: Sunflower seeds are used for oil extraction and food products.

Environmental Importance: Sunflowers attract pollinators and help improve soil quality.

Additional Facts: Sunflowers symbolize positivity and energy.
