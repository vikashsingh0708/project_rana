Rose Plant

Scientific Name: Rosa

Family: Rosaceae

Type: Flowering Plant

Description: Rose is a well-known flowering plant famous for its beauty, fragrance, and variety of colors. It is widely grown in gardens and parks across the world.

Uses: Roses are used for decoration, making perfumes, rose water, cosmetics, and in traditional medicines.

Environmental Importance: Rose plants support pollinators such as bees and butterflies. They help in maintaining biodiversity and improve air quality.

Additional Facts: Roses are symbols of love and peace. They require sunlight and well-drained soil for healthy growth.
