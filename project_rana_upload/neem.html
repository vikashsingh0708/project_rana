Neem Tree

Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica

Family: Meliaceae

Type: Medicinal Tree

Description: Neem is a fast-growing tree known for its strong antibacterial and antifungal properties.

Uses: Neem is used in medicines, toothpaste, skin care products, and natural pesticides.

Environmental Importance: Neem improves air quality and controls harmful insects naturally.

Additional Facts: Almost every part of the neem tree has medicinal value.
